About Us.

Specialized provider of implementation solutions for healthcare business processes

Juxt Rx is a specialized provider of implementation solutions for healthcare business processes, with a strong focus on pharmacy operations, pharmacy benefit management, and consultancy services. Our mission is to empower healthcare organizations by delivering seamless and cutting-edge solutions that optimize business processes, drive operational excellence, and elevate patient care. Working closely with our clients, we deliver customized and advanced solutions that improve efficiency, reduce costs, and foster positive outcomes in the ever-evolving healthcare industry.

Whether you need help with patient registration and admission, medical billing and coding, or any other healthcare business process, our team of experts is here to help. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, and we work closely with our clients to develop tailored solutions that meet their unique needs. Let us help you transform your healthcare business processes and take your organization to the next level.

Our Value Addition

At Juxt Rx, we pride ourselves on delivering comprehensive and customized solutions that address the evolving needs of the healthcare industry. With a strong focus on pharmacy operations, consultancy services, brand management, and regulatory support, we are dedicated to transforming healthcare businesses and driving positive outcomes for our clients.

Pharmacy Operations, and Consultancy Services

1. Pharmacy Operations: Juxt Rx offers comprehensive consultancy services to pharmacies, providing guidance on optimizing inventory management, implementing workflow efficiencies, and enhancing patient engagement. We assist pharmacies in leveraging technology to automate processes, improve prescription processing, and ultimately deliver better patient outcomes.

2. Pharmacy Benefit Management (PBM): Our PBM services encompass end-to-end management of prescription drug benefits for health insurance plans and employers. With a focus on cost containment and quality care, we negotiate with pharmaceutical manufacturers to secure the best pricing for medications, while ensuring access to a wide range of drugs for plan members.

3. Consultancy Services: Juxt Rx's consultancy services extend to healthcare organizations seeking expert advice on navigating complex industry challenges. From improving adherence to regulatory compliance, our consultants provide valuable insights and strategic recommendations to drive positive outcomes and business growth.

Local Trade Representative (LTR) and Brand Management

Regulatory Support: Navigating the regulatory landscape can be complex and time-consuming, particularly for pharmaceutical companies entering new markets. Juxt Rx offers regulatory support services that assist in obtaining necessary approvals, certifications, and product registrations in compliance with local regulations. Our team ensures a smooth market entry process, enabling companies to bring their products to market efficiently and in adherence to all applicable regulatory requirements.

Brand Management: Additionally, our brand management expertise helps in positioning products effectively and building a strong brand identity that resonates with the target audience.

Startup Services: Entrepreneurs and startups entering the healthcare industry face unique challenges.
